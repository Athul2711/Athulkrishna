SightSense Smart Glass is an AI-powered assistive device for the visually impaired. HandWave Glove Controler

Our team developed the HandWave Glove Controller, a PC controller for individuals with partial paralysis. By using wireless communication, web APIs, accelerometers, and flex sensors, we captured and transmitted hand and finger movements to control a computer. This innovation allows disabled individuals to game, work, and use a computer like everyone else. It was a great team experience.
